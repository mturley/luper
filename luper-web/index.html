NOTE: Your device must be set to allow installation of applications from "Unknown Sources" (found in Settings > Security)


Installation Instructions

  1. Navigate to this page (TeamLuper.com) in your Android device's browser.
  2. Download LuperAlpha.apk directly to your device by touching the icon at left.
  3. When the download is complete, pull down your notifications pane and touch the LuperAlpha.apk Download Complete notification. You will be prompted to install the application.

OR

  1. Download LuperAlpha.apk on your PC and copy it to your Android device's storage.
  2. Make sure you have a File Manager application on your device, such as ASTRO (available on Google Play)
  3. Using your File Manager, navigate to and open the LuperAndroid.apk file. You will be prompted to install the application.
